Summer is perhaps the most restful season to visit the cabin. The weather is almost always sunny and warm, with cool and invting Lake Mary only a few feet away. Activities include hiking, mountain biking, swimming, canoeing, rock climbing, tanning, and, of course, sitting on the porch and watching the world go by...
