Dartmouth Outing Club of Northern California Young Alum Festival

When: Weekend of August 27th-29th, 1999

Who: All interested D alums minted within the past 10 years

Where: The DOCNC Cabin in the Sierra

The big cabin weekend is fast approaching!

We've already received MANY responses. To better gauge exactly how big our weekend will be, we're asking everyone who is planning on attending to please let us know.

The purposes of this weekend at DOCNC's beautiful Donner Summit cabin are to get you out into them there woods, and to educate you a bit about the proper care, feeding, and historical background of our beloved cabin and the club which runs it. Not only that, but you may find yourself learning a thing or three about the surrounding mountains, too!

Staying at the cabin will be FREE for the weekend. Food/Drink will be on a BYOF/BYOB basis and we hope it turns into something resembling a potluck smorgasbord. (For more info on the cabin/etc., please check out http://www.alum.dartmouth.org/clubs/docnc/docnc.html)

Please email Kevin and/or Jason if you plan on joining us for the cabin

weekend or if you have any questions. More details to come to those who rsvp, otherwise this will be the last you'll get from us.

Hope to hear back from everyone!

- Kevin & Jason, on behalf of the DOCNC
